Discover the Story of BonBon Matcha

Using 100% ceremonial-grade matcha sourced from Japan, we craft exceptional drinks, gelato, cakes, cheesecakes, tiramisu, and seasonal desserts that celebrate the depth, aroma, and complexity of authentic matcha. From our signature Matcha Latte and Matcha Gelato to our creamy Matcha Basque Cheesecake, Matcha Tiramisu and Double Matcha Cake, every creation is designed to deliver a rich and memorable matcha experience. Located in the heart of Silicon Valley, BonBon Matcha is a destination for matcha enthusiasts, dessert lovers, and anyone seeking a unique Japanese-inspired café experience
